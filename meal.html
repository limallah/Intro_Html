Steps or Method of preparation for Semovita
  1. Set the water on stove (the amount you desired)
  2. When steaming i.e. hot add a pinch of salt and a table spoon of vegetable oil(optional)
  3. Fetch small portion of semovita powder and mix in a bowl with ordinary water
  4. When the water start boiling use your turning stick and pour in the mix semo powder
  5. After thicking a bit, leave it to boil for 15-20 min on a moderate heat
  6. Fetch out of the powder and start turning it till you get your desired smoothness and you can live it on the heat for another 2 min but on a very low heat
Steps or Method of preparation for Okro Soup
  • Any of the mentioned meat you are using wash it properly and bring to boil
  • Add your salt, onions, season and any other spices like powder garlic or ginger
  • Wash your okro and cut it in to small chops or use your greater to achieve the cut
  • Blend your onions, peppers, crayfish, locust bean or pound them using pistle and moatar. (each seperately is advicable)
  • Set your pot on the heat allow to heat up a little and pour in the palm oil
  • Allow to heat up a little and add in the pepper, onions, crayfish and locust bean
  • Allow to fry for like 2 min on a moderate heat, add okro and and allow to fry on low heat too. Don't forget to be stiring it till you observed its sleepery
  • Now add your stock water, potash water, meat too and stir still your desired thickness
  • Add 1-2 cube of season, taste it if there is need for salt and allow to steam for 2 min
